Cyberia Restaurant transports you to a nostalgic world of old retro anime, where every detail is infused with the charm of a bygone era. Step into our time capsule as we open our doors for the first time, under the visionary guidance of our founder, William Afton.

Drawing inspiration from classic anime aesthetics, Cyberia Restaurant immerses you in a captivating ambiance reminiscent of your favorite shows. The walls adorned with vibrant manga artwork and the retro-themed decor create an atmosphere that sparks joy and excitement.

Our menu pays homage to beloved anime series, featuring dishes that capture the essence of iconic characters and their adventures. From delightful ramen bowls inspired by spirited ninjas to whimsical desserts that resemble magical creatures, each bite is a nostalgic journey.

At Cyberia, we believe that dining should be an immersive experience. Our passionate staff, dressed in cosplay uniforms, are ready to guide you through a gastronomic adventure like no other. Prepare to be transported to a world where imagination and flavor collide.

Join us as we embark on this thrilling anime-themed culinary escapade. Discover the fusion of delectable flavors, impeccable service, and the visionary spirit of William Afton at Cyberia Restaurant.
